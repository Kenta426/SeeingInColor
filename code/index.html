Seeing in Color

This interface shows the color distribution of movie posters by metrics including genre, year, and primary/supplementary colors. The primary colors are the most prominent shades and hues on a poster, while the supplementary colors can be seen as complementary to the primary colors.

An analysis of movie poster color trends over the ages. The top 250 movies are collected each year from 1967 to 2016.

The bigger the circle/bar, the more popular the color is.

Color Thief was used to extract the dominant/representative color palette from the movie poster.

Data from Letterboxd and Color Thief.
